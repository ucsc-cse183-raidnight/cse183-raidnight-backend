Raidnight

A better way to find time to play with your friends.

Intuitive.

Quick to set up and intuitive to use, Raidnight will find the time that works best for you and your friends. Built to help bring friends together to play Final Fantasy 14, Raidnight offers presets to get you started in seconds.

Smart.

Who's the healer again? No more shuffling roles - Raidnight finds not just the best time, but the best role for each person to play, too.

Customizable.

Don't play FFXIV? The role system is completely customizable - you can use Raidnight for D&D, WoW, meetings, or any other time you might need to bring together a group of people!
